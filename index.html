What's included, out of the box

Training Management (TMS)

Coordinate logistics, schedule live instructor-led training (ILT) sessions, allocate instructors, and manage compliance tracking inside a centralized TMS interface designed for scaling operations.

Learning Management (LMS)

Support learners through multiple paths: self-paced digital courseware, live Virtual Instructor-Led Training (vILT) classrooms, or hybrid physical ILT sessions. Manage and distribute content instantly.

Branded Learner Portal

Provide learners with a localized, custom-branded experience. Deliver course catalogs, student registrations, and portal dashboards hosted directly on your private domain.

Progress & Certification

Verify and generate digital course certifications automatically on completion. Access performance graphs, export auditor-ready compliance data, and track student milestones.
